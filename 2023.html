Because of my knitting club, I was meeting many more crocheters than I had ever before. Since the technique is deemed "easier" than knitting by many, people came to my knitting club with the hopes of learning how to crochet.

Thus, the smart thing to do was to learn how to crochet of course.

For my first crochet project then, I had this dream of having a patchwork cardigan since at the time, it was quite popular. It seemed easy enough since all I had to do was make a bunch of squares.

Although I had prior experience with knitting, learning how to crochet was a fun experience, and I left the project eager to improve for future projects!
