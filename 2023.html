Since Crimson Robotics hosts the local FTC competition for our area, I was used to volunteering for the event every Winter. Although, when my friend approached me about being an emcee for the matches with him, I was definitely surprised.

I wasn't resistant because of my surprise though, and I easily agreed since I loved the idea of being able to have fun with my friend and giving that energy to the event.

I thought it was a fun experience getting to know the inner works of the matches; for example, how they score or determine winners. More than that though, I had a great time analyzing all the different robots on such a close proximity.

Going from competing in FTC to being photographer to emcee, I'm really grateful to have the opportunity to see our event in so many perspectives. Because not only did I see the event differently, I got to see all of the effort every party puts in for our FTC competitions.
