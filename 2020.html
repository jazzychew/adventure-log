During the summer of 2020, it's not hard to understand my boredom. Looking for volunteer opportunities, I stumbled upon Funetix's initiative.

At the time, they were developing their reading app & program that would expand literacy to many diverse communities.

Furthermore, being a hobby artist, I saw that they offered artists an opportunity to support the cause & submit artwork to be used on their merchandise.

Especially as literature has been a saving grace in my life, I had to draw something.

Although it's quite amateur, I'm proud of my efforts which you can see above!
